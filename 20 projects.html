20% Projects

What are 20% Time Projects?

20% Projects are a way to enjoy technology. Based of off google's 20% Time, students are allowed to work on their own projects for 20% of their class time as a way for them to explore creative ways to use technology. You will find my 20% Time projects here.

Trimester A 20% Time Project

In this 20% Time Project I explored Unity and it's variety of tools and features for the building of games. Unity has many tools and features that are designed not only for games, but also for 3D design, and even has the capability to be used as a simulation software.

What hardware and software was used?

A macbook, Google Chrome, and Unity were used.

What was my biggest challenge?

My biggest challenge was figuring out how to use different tools.

What was my most favorite part?

Personally, my favorite part of this project was the tile mapping process.

What did I learn?

During the production of this project, I learned how to use Unity's toolset.
